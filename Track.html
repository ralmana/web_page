Track and Field

Track and Field is my favorite sport. I love it because it makes me test how far I can go in my goals I set for myself, and how to overcome challenges strategically. Below I listed the 2 main events that I do which in my opinion are the most mentally strategic ones. To learn more about Track and Field, look at the World Athletics website.

Long Distance Running

Long distance races typically range from 800 meters to the marathon (42.195 kilometers). These events test endurance, pace strategy, and mental toughness.

Throwing Events

Throwing competitions in Track and Field include the shot put, discus throw, hammer throw, and javelin throw. Each event has unique techniques and requires strength, technique, and precision.
