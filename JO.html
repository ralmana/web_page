Jordan

Jordan, a fascinating country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty, is located in the heart of the Middle East. Known for its ancient monuments, nature reserves, and seaside resorts, it's a land of awe-inspiring landscapes and stories.

Jordan is home to several UNESCO World Heritage sites, including the ancient city of Petra, the Nabatean caravan-city that is half-built, half-carved into the rock, and the desert landscape of Wadi Rum, known for its red sands, stunning rock formations, and petroglyphs.
